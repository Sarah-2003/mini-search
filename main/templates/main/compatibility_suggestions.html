Explanation of the Big-5 Personality Traits (OCEAN)

  • Openness: Describes the degree of intellectual curiosity, creativity, and preference for novelty and variety.
  • Conscientiousness: Refers to self-discipline, act dutifully, and aim for achievement.
  • Extraversion: Involves energy, positive emotions, and the tendency to seek stimulation and the company of others.
  • Agreeableness: Reflects individual differences in general concern for social harmony.
  • Neuroticism: Tendency to experience unpleasant emotions easily.

Description of How AI-Driven Matching Works

  • Users complete a personality test based on the Big-5 traits.
  • The AI analyzes the personality profiles and identifies potential matches.
  • Users receive suggestions based on compatibility scores.
