Receive personalized location-based recommendations.

How Location-Based Recommendations Are Generated

  • Users input their interests and preferred types of venues (e.g., cafes, parks, museums).
  • The platform analyzes user preferences and location data.
  • The algorithm suggests venues that align with the mutual interests of matched users.

Example Suggestions and User Scenarios

  • Example 1: Two users interested in art receive a recommendation to visit a local art gallery.
  • Example 2: Users who enjoy outdoor activities get a suggestion to meet at a nearby park.
